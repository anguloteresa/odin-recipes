Banoffee Pavlova

A baked meringue is topped with banana slices, caramel sauce and whipped cream.

This is a delicious combination of two very yummy desserts making one that will have you making it over and over again!!!

Ingredients

  • Castor sugar
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 pinch cream of tartar
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 dash vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 small bananas
  • 1 cup caramel ice cream topping
  • 1 tsp semisweet chocolate, grated
  • 2 cups sweetened whipped cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Make pavlova
  3. Bake the pavlova
  4. Decorate the pavlova
